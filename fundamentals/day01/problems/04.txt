Question 4
Accept the age of a person and check whether he/she is eligible to vote or not. A person is eligible to vote only when he/she is 18 years or more.

Algorithm

Step 1 - Start
Step 2 - Input the age of the person (age)
Step 3 - If age is greater than or equal to 18, then
Step 4 - Display "You are eligible to vote"
Step 5 - Else
Step 6 - Display "You are not eligible to vote"
Step 7 - End

Pseudo-code

Start
Input age
if (age >= 18) then
    Display "You are eligible to vote"
else
    Display "You are not eligible to vote"
End
